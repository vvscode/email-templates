Go mobile with the Collectrium app

Now that you're familiar with the basics of Collectrium on the web, let’s download the Collectrium Mobile App and connect it to your account.

Remember to download the complimentary Collectrium iPhone and iPad companion app to access your art collection from anywhere you are, even offline while traveling.

Add your first object:

1. On your iPhone or iPad, go to the App Store and search for "Collectrium." Click on Get to install the app.
2. Launch the app and log in using the same username and password that you used to create your account.
3. You can now access your collection anytime, anywhere, through your mobile devices. Fill out the remaining description fields, add images, fill in financial information, and more.

The Collectrium App is fully mobile-optimized and offline-ready, so you can access your collection even when you don’t have an internet connection. When you re-connect, the app will automatically sync any changes.
